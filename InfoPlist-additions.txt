Add these keys to your Info.plist (Xcode: target → Info tab, or edit Info.plist source):

1. NSLocalNetworkUsageDescription (String)
   Value: "Sorriva needs access to your local network to discover and control Sonos and Bluesound speakers."

2. NSBonjourServices (Array of Strings)
   Items:
   - _sonos._tcp
   - _musc._tcp
   - _musp._tcp
   - _musz._tcp
   - _mush._tcp
